MOFU ROTHENBURG ORGANIZATION

Mofu Rothenburg Organization is a dynamic and forward thinking non-governmental organization based in Tanzania. Our organization aims to improve life of people in Tanzania by supporting their efforts to get much better life by provision of life skills through education, vocational trainings, food security, agriculture support, health care, environmental care, technology and sporting. Our organization headquarters is at Mofu Village within Kilombero District in Morogoro Region. The organization has been dully registered under the Non Governmental Organizations Act, 2002 with Registration No: 00NGO/R/3872 to operate in  Tanzanian Mainland in accordance with its governing constistution and the laws of Tanzania.

Our mission

We are focusing on empowering community to bring vital changes that tackle the root causes of poverty and ignorance which undermines their development through social awareness, community engagement, education support and empowerment.

our vision

 Mofu Rothenburg Organization vision is to be a leading organization for social and economic development, driven by community awareness, social inclusion, community engagement, advocacy and empowerment.

Our Values

At Mofu Rothenburg Organization, our core values are the compass that guides our actions, decisions, and interactions. These values represent the foundation of our shape of our commitment towards community empowerment.

Awareness

We believe in raising awareness about education challenges, food security, environmental issues, agriculture, sanitation and hygiene and the importance of sustainable living. Awareness is the first step towards positive change.

Transparency

This is vital in our work. We maintain open and honest communication with our stakeholders ensuring trust and accountability.

Innovation

This is a driving force behind our solutions. We continuously seek creative and forward-thinking approaches to environmental management and community empowerment.

Accountability

We hold ourselves accountable for our commitments and other outcomes of our initiatives.

Our objectives

  • Empower the youth’s living in difficult condition access to education
  • Advocate for access to food for primary and secondary school students in the community
  • Facilitate the increase of access to better agriculture, post-harvest storage technologies and handling facilities, value chain and addition, market facilitation, community systems strengthening, nutrition, food security and institutional capacity development to the communities.
  • Empower the community’s access to and use of improved water, sanitation, hygiene services and facilities and with changed sanitation and hygiene behaviors at scale in the community.
  • Strengthen community’s entrepreneurship and community village serving groups.
  • Promotion of environmental conservation and proper utilization of environmental resources.
  • Advocate to access to justice and protecti on of human rights.
