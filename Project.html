PROJECTS IMPLEMENTED BY MOFU ROTHENBURG ORGANIZATION

Mofu Rothenburg Vocational Training Center
The organization in cooperation with Mofu Rothenburg Association of Switzerland has been able to construct a vocational training center and the organizational head office. With the aim of helping people living in difficult conditions especially young people and women, this center is offering various vocational training courses such as basic tailoring, basic electrical installation, basic welding, masonry and brick laying course. In the near future the Organization expects to introduce other courses like agriculture, computer application, carpentry, food production, hair dressing and beautification. This Vocational Training Center is located at Mpakani Street in Ihenga Village within Mofu Ward in Kilombero District, Morogoro Region. Current courses which are offered in the vocational training center include the following:-

Basic Tailoring

In cooperation with Mofu Rothenburg Association of Switzerland the organization is offering a six m​​onths tailoring course to the young women and men of Mofu. In this course the students learn to how make clothes and mend them easily. The course is fully funded by Mofu Rothenburg Association of Switzerland.

Basic Electricians

The organization is offering a six months basic course for electrical trainings to the young women and men of Mofu. At the end of the course the students will be able to carry out simple installations as independent professionals. The course is fully funded by Mofu Rothenburg Association of Switzerland.

Basic Masons/bricklayers

The organization is offering a six months basic course for house building to the young men and women of Mofu. At the end of the course the students will be able to carry out building work as professional Masons. The course is fully funded by Mofu Rothenburg Association of Switzerland.

Basic welding

The organization is offering a six months basic course for welding training to the young men and women of Mofu. The course offers students to learn basic techniques of welding. At the end of the course the students will be able to carry out welding activities as professional welders. The course is fully funded by Mofu Rothenburg Association of Switzerland.

Agriculture projects

Mofu Rothenburg Organization in cooperation with Mofu Rothenburg Association of Switzerland works with farming communities to introduce sustainable farming practices that increase agricultural output. We are working with the Union of farmer’s groups in Mofu (UVIWAMO). There are about eight (8) groups of Mofu local rice farmers consists of 146 members with 479 dependants whom we provide them knowledge in sustainable agricultural farming with small grants to boost their rice farming activities.

Water projects

Mofu Rothenburg Organization has been advocating for provision of clean and safe water to the communities. In cooperation with Mofu Rothenburg Association in Switzerland and other development partners has been able to fund the provision of safe and clean water to six primary schools, one secondary school and one vocational training center in Mofu through water filter project. Through this project, the students have been able to get clean and safe water and hence reduce the number of diseases associated with water.

Provision of School meals

The organization is also providing sugar to all primary schools in Mofu Ward which allows students to drink porridge throughout the year. This project has not only reduce the number of drop out students within Mofu but also enables students to get something to eat in the morning and which allows them to concentrate more in the studies and hence led to the improve and increase their academic performance. In this project, the organization provides sugar and pays for cooks salaries while the parents contributed maize and rice.

Students living in difficult condition

The organization has been supporting a number of students living in difficult condition through payment of their school fees or tuition fees in all levels that is from secondary, college and university level.

The organization also provide supports students living in difficult condition who are studying at Mofu Secondary School by providing them with school items.

The organization also through various donations from Mofu Rothenburg Association of Switzerland has been able to provide 200 tables, 200 chairs, 24 beds, 1 Generator, 1 Photocopy machine and 1 lap top to Mofu Secondary School to help the students study in proper environment. 

Group projects

The organization has also been supporting group projects. We have supported Mofu Water For Life Group by sending two members to school to learn about well drilling, plumbing and pipe fitting. We have also bought them the equipments for water drilling and now they are practicing their profession in Mofu ward.

Mofu Rothenburg Organization has been making efforts to supports the efforts of various groups in order to get rid of themselves economically and get rid of poverty, one of those groups is Jikwamue Group which we have supported in agriculture and finishing of the houses they are building for each other within the group. 
Mapendo group
Mapendo Group, a group of loving mothers, engaged in cooking. Mofu Rothenburg Organization has provided them with cooking equipment to enable them to become economically self-sufficient by offering catering services. 

                   Water for all Group

Water for all Group, a group of young people in Mofu, engaged in drilling wells and installation of water systems. Mofu Rothenburg Organization has provided them with well drilling equipments to enable them to become economically self-sufficient by offering water services. 

Environme​ntal friendly projects

The organization also supports environmental projects like bees keeping project, in cooperation with Mofu Rothenburg Association of Switzerland, we have been able to support Mokonyo bees keeping group, and this is a local group in Mofu. We are also open to all other environmental projects which fit our organizational objectives.

Religious projects

Through various contributions from different donors the organization has been able to do renovation activities to the Mofu Parish Catholic Church, Mofu Parish Priest’s house, reconstruction of the catechist house, renovation of Mofu Ward Masjid and Ihenga Masjid.

Strengthen Youth Voice (Sauti ya Vijana Project-SYV).

Mofu Rothenburg Organization in partnership with Women’s Global Network for Reproductive Rights (WGNNR-AFRICA) engages in advocacy for comprehensive sexual reproductive health services, aiming to strengthen young people's voices and ensure access to vital services and information.
